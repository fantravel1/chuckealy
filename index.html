The Legend

Three Chapters. Zero Losses.

Every era of Chuck Ealey's career is a story of dominance, resilience, and defying the odds.

Toledo Rockets 1969-1971

35-0: The Record That Still Stands

Three undefeated seasons. Three MAC titles. Three Tangerine Bowl MVPs. 8th in Heisman voting. The most dominant run by a quarterback in college football history.

5,275 passing yards 45 TDs
1972 NFL Draft

442 Picks. Zero for Ealey.

All 26 NFL teams passed on the undefeated quarterback across 17 rounds. His crime? Refusing to play anything other than QB at a time when Black quarterbacks weren't welcome.

Refused to switch positions Stood his ground
Grey Cup Champion 1972

First Black QB to Win the Grey Cup

Signed with the Hamilton Tiger-Cats. Led a 68-yard drive in the final two minutes. Won the championship. Won MVP. As a rookie. The NFL's loss became Canada's greatest gain.

Grey Cup MVP 291 yards, 1 TD
