The Chuck Ealey Foundation

In 2006, Chuck Ealey established the Chuck Ealey Foundation with a singular mission: to pass the torch of the Undefeated Spirit to the next generation. Rooted in the same values that carried him from the streets of Portsmouth, Ohio to the Grey Cup championship and the College Football Hall of Fame, the Foundation operates on the belief that every young person deserves the tools, encouragement, and opportunity to succeed.

The Foundation's centerpiece is the Undefeated Spirit Award — an annual scholarship program that recognizes high school and college students who demonstrate perseverance, academic commitment, community involvement, and the refusal to quit regardless of the obstacles they face.

Operating primarily across Canada and the United States, the Foundation partners with schools, community organizations, and corporate sponsors to fund scholarships, mentorship programs, and youth leadership events. It is not about football. It is about giving young people the confidence to believe in themselves.

Scholarships

The Undefeated Spirit Award

Each year, the Chuck Ealey Foundation presents the Undefeated Spirit Award to outstanding high school and college students who embody the values of perseverance, integrity, and academic dedication.

The Undefeated Spirit Award is not about athletic ability. It is about character. Recipients are young people who have faced significant adversity — poverty, family hardship, health challenges, systemic barriers — and have refused to let those obstacles define their future. They have maintained academic excellence, contributed to their communities, and demonstrated the kind of spirit that Chuck himself showed when 26 NFL teams told him no.

Award recipients receive scholarships to support their post-secondary education, mentorship from Chuck and Foundation board members, and recognition at an annual awards ceremony that celebrates their achievements in front of their families, peers, and community leaders.

Since the Foundation's inception, dozens of students have been recognized and supported through the Undefeated Spirit Award, with many going on to graduate from university, launch careers, and give back to their own communities — carrying the Undefeated Spirit forward.

Bring the Undefeated Spirit to Your Event

Chuck Ealey is available for keynote addresses, corporate leadership events, school assemblies, community fundraisers, and private speaking engagements across North America.

Keynote Address

Chuck's full keynote, "The 3 Keys to a Winning Edge," is a 45-to-90 minute presentation that covers his journey from Portsmouth to the Grey Cup, the NFL snub, and the life lessons that came from never losing.

School Assembly

Tailored for K-12 and university audiences, Chuck's school presentations focus on education, perseverance, and making positive choices. Interactive Q&A sessions are included.

Corporate Event

Chuck connects his athletic journey to leadership, teamwork, and resilience in the workplace. Perfect for leadership summits, team retreats, and annual conferences.

Community & Fundraiser

Chuck regularly supports community causes and fundraising events. His presence elevates any event and his story creates an emotional connection that drives engagement and giving.
