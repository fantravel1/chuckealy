FAQ

Common Questions

Straight answers to the questions I hear most often.

What does a typical engagement look like?

It depends on the model. Advisory engagements are typically 2-4 hours per month of strategic guidance. Fractional leadership is 2-3 days per week embedded in your organization. Speaking engagements are scoped per event. Partnership builds are long-term commitments with equity alignment. Every engagement starts with a discovery call to define scope and fit.

How quickly can you start?

For advisory work, I can typically begin within one to two weeks of our initial conversation. Fractional leadership roles require a more structured onboarding and usually start within 30 days. I maintain limited availability intentionally to ensure every client gets my full attention.

What industries do you work in?

My experience spans technology, SaaS, professional services, healthcare, and financial services. But the principles of operational excellence, team architecture, and strategic execution are industry-agnostic. If you have a complex problem and a willingness to execute, we can likely work together.

How do you measure success?

We define success metrics before we start. Every engagement has clear KPIs, documented baselines, and regular measurement cadences. I am not interested in engagements where success is subjective. If we cannot measure it, we are not doing it.

What if it is not a good fit?

I will tell you. The discovery call exists to determine mutual fit, and I turn down more engagements than I accept. If I am not the right person for your challenge, I will say so directly and point you toward someone who is. No hard feelings, no wasted time.
